About the Project

The “SafeScope” project aims to revolutionize occupational safety through the integration of advanced Artificial Intelligence solutions in hazardous environments, particularly in construction and industrial zones.

One of the central goals of the project is to ensure that employees wear Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) such as helmets, safety vests, gloves, and goggles at all times. By using real-time computer vision, the system can automatically verify the presence and correctness of this equipment without manual supervision.

Moreover, SafeScope introduces a smart proximity detection system which constantly monitors the distance between employees and potentially dangerous machinery or vehicles. This AI-driven feature acts as an early warning system and significantly reduces the risk of collisions or workplace accidents.

The visual detection model employed by SafeScope is based on deep learning techniques that are trained on thousands of annotated images. It uses object detection and pose estimation technologies to accurately interpret human presence and interaction with machinery and their surroundings.

The system not only detects violations but also records them in a central database for compliance tracking and future analysis. Supervisors can access these logs to identify patterns, optimize workflows, and offer targeted training where safety breaches are most common.

Another distinguishing feature of SafeScope is its scalability and flexibility. Whether it’s a small warehouse or a large construction site, the system can be easily deployed using standard IP cameras and lightweight hardware. This makes it a cost-effective and practical solution for companies of all sizes.

In the long term, SafeScope is designed to not only improve workplace safety but also foster a culture of proactive risk management. With the integration of dashboards, real-time alerts, and compliance reports, the project transforms how organizations think about safety — from reactive to predictive.

By combining modern artificial intelligence, automation, and data analytics, SafeScope represents a major step forward in the evolution of occupational health and safety technologies.
